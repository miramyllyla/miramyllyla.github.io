Who am I?

My name is Mira Myllylä and I am a third-year student from Aalto University School of Science. My major is Information Networks and I have a passion for UI and UX design. Problem solving and the eternal question “how to make this even better?” make these subjects very hooking. My experiences in working life have thought me how to build strong relationships with all departments at an organization. I have the ability to work within teams as well as cross-team. I know I can work with web engineers to solve technical issues but also communicate with the marketing department.

My way of working

At my current job I get to work with people from different cultures and backgrounds mostly in English. That is something I truly appreciate and I am sure it has a great effect on how I work. Also, my strong background from customer service has taught me a lot, both in working and in personal life. I have a constant desire to learn new skills and my open and self-driven personality enables me to adapt new ways of working really fast. My co-workers have described me as “fun, purposeful, conscientious and easy to work with”. My aim has always been to work diligently but also have fun with co-workers whenever it is possible.

Hobbies & interests

I love playing with Adobe Software like Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign and Premiere, even though I also use them daily at my current work and at school. When I need to relax, I usually go for a run or do yoga. During winter I ski as much as I can!

Good food and especially baking are very close to my heart and I even have my own bakery as a part time hobby. If you are in a need of good cake, call me!
